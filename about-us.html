About Us

DAL Investments Ltd (DAL) is a professionally managed Investment Company that invests proprietary money of Mr Suryakant Dalmia in both listed and unlisted commercial enterprises. We are based out of Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC), Dubai.

DAL invests in new age innovative sectors that have the potential to make an impact on business environment or consumer behavior. We also specialize in various exchange traded financial derivatives instruments. We also recognize the importance of cryptocurrency as a new world asset class.

Our current focus is in new age sectors like Big Data, Plant Proteins, Clean Energy, Cloud, AI & ML, Platforms, Fintech, Blockchain Technology & New World Materials to name a few.
