About Us

With the strategic importance of UAE growing by the day in the global arena, a significant amount of decision making is converging in this country for various investment decisions across the spectrum – be it starting or expanding businesses, mergers and acquisitions, estate planning or wealth management.

One cannot overemphasize the significance to taking a prudent investment decision with the right balance between return maximization and risk minimization. With this philosophy in mind, DAL Investments LLC FZ (DAL) started its business operations in August 2023 with the primary objective of providing Financial Consulting and Analysis.

DAL has a collective experience of more than 50 man year with its expertize being in the field financial services spanning various asset classes like Equities, Corporate Debt, Currency, Commodities and Precious Metals among others.
